This is a multi-perspective design review for consensus.

Topic: Plan a technical migration from monolithic service to microservices

Participants:
1. Architect: Focuses on system design, scalability, and technical debt
2. Operations: Considers deployment, monitoring, incident response, and reliability
3. Product: Represents business impact, timeline, and customer experience

Objective: Reach consensus on a migration plan covering:
1. Target architecture (which services to break out first, why)
2. Phasing strategy (timeline and dependencies)
3. Key risks and mitigation approaches
4. Success metrics and rollback strategy
5. Team and resource requirements

Collaboration style:
- Each participant brings critical perspective without dismissing others
- Look for creative solutions that satisfy multiple constraints
- Surface hidden assumptions early
- Test ideas against real-world scenarios
- Identify when there's genuine trade-off vs. win-win

Target: Consensus on plan within 8 turns. If disagreements remain, clearly list options and trade-offs for stakeholders.
