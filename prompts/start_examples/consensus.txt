Your task is to reach consensus on the design of a new software system.

Topic: Design a rate-limiting system for a high-traffic API

Participants:
1. Analyst: focuses on requirements and trade-offs
2. Creative: proposes unconventional solutions
3. Researcher: validates against industry best practices

Objective:
1. Identify 5 key requirements for the rate limiter
2. Evaluate 3 different approaches (token bucket, sliding window, leaky bucket)
3. Recommend a single best approach with justification
4. Identify potential gotchas and how to handle them

Each participant should contribute their unique perspective. The goal is consensus, not debate--focus on building on each other's ideas rather than arguing.

Target: Reach consensus on recommendation within 8 turns.
